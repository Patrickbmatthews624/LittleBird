Our Story

A quiet sanctuary on the East End

Nestled in the heart of Southampton, Little Bird Healing Spa offers therapeutic massage, restorative facials, and expert waxing. All rooted in traditional Chinese healing practices and delivered with genuine warmth.

Owner Jacqui and her skilled team treat every guest with the utmost care. No pretense, no frills. Just exceptional hands-on work that keeps our guests coming back year after year.
